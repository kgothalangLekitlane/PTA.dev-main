The Vortrekker Monument

The Vortrekker Monument is a symbol of the Great Trek and the pioneers who moved into the interior of South Africa during the 1830s and 1840s. Erected in 1949, it commemorates the courage and determination of the Voortrekkers. The monument features a striking architectural design and offers breathtaking views of Pretoria.
